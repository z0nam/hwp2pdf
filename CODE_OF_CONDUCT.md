# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to keep this project practical, respectful, and welcoming for users and contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially when diagnosing environment-specific Windows or Hancom Office issues.
- Keep issue discussions focused on reproducible behavior and useful context.
- Avoid sharing confidential documents or personal information in public issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated off-topic or hostile comments
- Sharing malicious files or exploit instructions outside responsible disclosure

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block participants who do not follow this code of conduct.
